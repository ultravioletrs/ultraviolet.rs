Ultraviolet Consult is a cloud computing company oriented towards secure and confidential computing technologies in the domain of telecom, cloud, and IoT systems.

Research Engineer

AI/ML, IoT and Security

Position Overview

Ultraviolet Consult is looking for a research engineer with a background in Telecommunication, Electronics and/or Computer Sciences, ambitious and interested to work with cutting-edge technologies like IoT, 5G, distributed systems, AI/ML and Confidential Computing (digital security).

Ultraviolet has been awarded by the Innovation Fund from the budget of the Republic of Serbia from the division of the Ministry of Education, Science and Technological Development to develop the CoCoS.ai project, together with ETF, University of Belgrade. The project will bring innovation in federated AI/ML orchestration with highest digital security and privacy-preserving procedures, and will be a unique solution of this kind.

Additionally, Ultraviolet is involved in large-scale EU research projects, like SPATIAL, TITAN or EDGEWARE, funded by the European Commission via Horizon 2020 and Horizon Europe programs, where it cooperates with the most prominent European universities, research institutes and companies in order to bring excellence and innovation in AI/ML, IoT Cloud and Edge computing and cybersecurity. Work is oriented around EU data strategy, European plan for AI, and the new EU Cybersecurity strategy, and it is done in coordination with other relevant national, regional and trans-national initiatives such as Gaia-X and EOSC, especially to ensure interoperability and reasonable re-use of common reference models, processes and building blocks for a pan-European data infrastructure.

Tasks

Research in the domain of IoT, Cloud and Edge computing, AI/ML orchestration and security issues, protocols and practices. Development of prototypes. Documentation and reporting. Cooperation with other researchers through meetings, brainstormings and workshops. Work on standardization and dissemination of results. Presenting at scientific conferences. Writing and publishing scientific papers.

Skills

  • PhD in Engineering (Telecommunication, Electronics, Computer Sciences or similar) or MSc and equivalent work experience
  • Knowledge and understanding of IoT networks and distributed systems
  • Cybersecurity and Confidential Computing
  • Familiarity with AI/ML

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary and benefit packages
  • Continuous professional development with support from experienced engineers and though the work on the large projects with most prominent tech companies in the world
  • Work with modern technologies and systems (future-tech)
  • Work in a dynamic and professional working environment
  • The possibility of work from home or remote destination
  • Flexible working time
  • Work within the international environment and participation on open-source projects

To apply for this position, please send your CV to e-mail: careers@ultraviolet.rs

About Ultraviolet Consult

Ultraviolet Consult is a technology company focused on Confidential Computing methodologies in the domain of Cloud, Telecom and AI/ML.

Company was founded in 2015. and has a proven track record working in the telecom space, cooperating with some of the world's biggest CSPs and operators - like Telenor, A1, Telia, Nokia, and many others - on design, installation and maintenance of 4G and 5G networks.

Ultraviolet is working on solving hardest security issues, protecting networks, data, and algorithms by applying the latest technological innovations in Confidential Computing, AI/ML and Secure Multi-Party Computation.
